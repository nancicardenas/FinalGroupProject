# Description:
Nine Lives is a puzzle stealth game where you are a cat navigating through the city trying to make it out without losing all of your 9 lives. Each time you reset or get caught by enemies, your previous run becomes a ghost that replays your actions. You are able to use your ghost cats to distract enemies, activate switches, and solve puzzles to reach the goal before running out of lives. Nine Lives will be a mobile game and will incorporate shaders.
